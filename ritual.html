Last updated: March 2026 Ritual Atlas is developed and maintained by Madzilla Apps. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use the Ritual Atlas app on Android. By using the app you agree to the practices described below. 1. Data We Collect Local data only Ritual Atlas stores all your personal data exclusively on your device. This includes your saved favorites, your onboarding status, and your premium purchase status. This data is never transmitted to our servers. Advertising ID The app requests access to your device's Advertising ID (AD_ID permission) so that Google AdMob can serve relevant ads to users who have not purchased the premium version. If you have not consented to personalised advertising, AdMob will show non-personalised ads only. Analytics We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated usage data (e.g. which screens are opened most often). This data does not identify you personally and is used solely to improve the app experience. Crash reports We use Google Firebase Crashlytics to receive anonymous crash and error reports. These reports help us identify and fix technical issues. They may include device model, OS version, and app state at the time of the crash, but no personally identifiable information. 2. Permissions We Use • Internet (android.permission.INTERNET) — required to load ads and send anonymous analytics and crash reports. • Advertising ID (com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID) — required on Android 13+ for AdMob to function. You can reset or opt out of ad personalisation in your device settings under Google → Ads. • Billing (com.android.vending.BILLING) — required to process the one-time premium purchase through Google Play. 3. Third-Party Services The following third-party SDKs are integrated into the app. Each operates under its own privacy policy. • Google AdMob — displays banner and interstitial ads to non-premium users. May collect device information and the Advertising ID for ad targeting. Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy • Google UMP (User Messaging Platform) — presents the consent dialog required by GDPR and other privacy regulations before initialising ads. • Google Play Billing — handles the one-time premium purchase. Purchase verification is performed by Google Play directly. • Google Firebase Analytics — collects anonymous usage statistics. • Google Firebase Crashlytics — collects anonymous crash reports. • Google Fonts / Preloaded Fonts — fonts are bundled or preloaded on-device; no network request is made at runtime for typography. • Coil — used for efficient on-device image loading from app resources. No network requests are made for images. 4. Advertising and Consent Non-premium users see ads served by Google AdMob. Before any personalised advertising is initialised, the app displays a consent form managed by Google UMP in compliance with GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California). You may update your consent choice at any time by re-opening the app after clearing its data, or by adjusting your device ad settings. Premium users do not see any ads. 5. In-App Purchases The app offers a single one-time purchase ("Premium") processed entirely through Google Play. We do not collect, store, or process any payment information. Google Play manages all billing and receipts. 6. Data Retention All app data (favorites, settings, premium status) is stored locally on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app. Anonymous analytics and crash data retained by Google are subject to Google's own retention policies. 7. Children's Privacy Ritual Atlas is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action. 8. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. 9.
